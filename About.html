Meet Our team

Elite Rehab and Performance is a private cash-based Physical Therapy clinic located in Scarsdale, NY. We personalize physical therapy care based on the needs of the individual to optimize their performance and function. We do this by focusing on a patient-centered and continual care approach. Elite Rehab and Performance offers private, one-on-one treatment sessions provided by one of our top-tier, board-certified Physical Therapists. Treatment sessions can be set anywhere from half hour to hour long sessions. We offer general orthopedic and neurologic treatment as well as specializations in running, strength and conditioning, and sports-specific training.

Corey McLeod

Corey is a Michigan native but has lived in various regions across the states, but recently made NY his home. After receiving his doctorate in physical therapy from Marist College, he settled in the Hudson Valley and became a co-founder at Elite Rehab and Performance. His interests include tennis and golf, distance trail running and cycling. He is passionate about always improving his hands-on manual skills and working with others to optimize performance.Aside from physical activity, his interests include spending time with his fiancée and two cats.

Michael Canino

Michael Canino received his bachelor's of science from Utica University and his Doctorate of Physical Therapy from Marist College in 2022. He has a background working with collegiate athlete's, especially baseball players. Michael has several years of experience doing both personal training and nutrition coaching. He believes that health and wellness is more than just being active. He believes it involves many aspects such as nutrition, physical activity, socialization, as well as mental health. You can expect a well rounded approach where you're being treated not as just a diagnosis on a prescription, but as a person. His passion is helping others return to their level of functioning prior to their injury or condition or to the next level performance that they strive for!

Andrew Mestman

Andrew was born in Santa Monica and went to school in Torrance, CA. He grew up in the beach cities — what the SoCal locals call “The South Bay” (Hermosa, Redondo, and Manhattan Beach). He received his Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from Cal State University of Domiguez Hills. He then went on to receive his Doctorate in Physical Therapy with high honors from Marist College in Poughkeepsie, NY. He has a background in manual therapy and certifications in sports performance. He believes integrating a hands on approach to treatment combined with corrective movements results in the highest quality of care for his patients. He spends his free time hiking the trails of Catskills, disk golfing, spending time with family, and looking for new gourmet cheeseburgers to try.

Gaby Rodriquez

Gabriela Rodriguez, P.T., D.P.T. graduated from the Marist College Doctor of Physical Therapy program in 2022. Prior to her career in Physical Therapy, Gabriela worked in what felt like an unfulfilling paralegal career in NYC. Ultimately, she decided that she wanted to change paths and focus on a more rewarding career that would provide a meaningful service to others. Gabriela chose Physical Therapy because it was the clearest convergence of her interests: fitness, life-long learning and most importantly, helping others. Working with patients and helping them meet their goals is easily her favorite part of the job. In her free time, Gabreila enjoys running, hiking and playing with her dog Bennie.
